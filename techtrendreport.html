The Hiring Pendulum: From Peak to Precision

The Indian IT industry has seen dramatic shifts in hiring. After a record-breaking surge in FY2021, net hiring has decelerated, indicating a strategic move towards efficiency and specialized, "just-in-time" recruitment over mass hiring.

The chart illustrates the sharp peak in net additions in FY2021 followed by a significant moderation, reflecting the industry's adaptation to a new economic climate.

Navigating Market Turbulence: Churn & Layoffs

Global Tech Layoffs (Employees Affected)

While India is part of a global trend of tech layoffs, driven by economic slowdowns and AI automation, its domestic job creation in high-demand areas has provided a degree of resilience.

Attrition's Ebb and Flow

Attrition rates dropped in 2024 as employees sought stability, but are rising again in FY25, heavily influenced by competitive offers from Global Capability Centres (GCCs).

Compensation Climb: Salary by Experience

Salaries in the Indian tech sector show a strong correlation with experience. While competitive within India, a significant gap remains compared to Western markets, influencing the "brain drain" of top talent.

This chart shows the average annual salary progression for a Software Developer in India. Specialized roles in AI/ML can command significantly higher packages at all levels.

Your Roadmap to Success in Tech

In today's competitive market, a strategic approach is key. Follow this path to enhance your employability and build a successful career in Indian tech.

1

Develop In-Demand Skills

Focus on high-growth areas like AI/ML, Cloud, and Cybersecurity. Build a portfolio of real-world projects and validate knowledge with certifications.

2

Build Your Online Brand

Your GitHub is your resume. Your LinkedIn is your network. Actively contribute to open-source, write articles, and engage with industry leaders.

3

Network Strategically

Connect with professionals online and at tech meetups. Informational interviews can open doors. Best opportunities often come through referrals.

4

Embrace Lifelong Learning

The tech landscape changes rapidly. Cultivate a mindset of continuous improvement. Stay curious, stay adaptable, and never stop learning.
